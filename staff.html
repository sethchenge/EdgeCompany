The Vision Behind EdgeTech

Seth Chenge founded EdgeTech in 2020 with a revolutionary vision to democratize technology solutions for businesses of all sizes. With over a decade of experience in systems engineering and enterprise architecture, Seth recognized the growing gap between complex technological needs and accessible IT solutions. His passion for innovation and commitment to excellence drove him to create a company that would bridge this gap through cutting-edge services and personalized support.

Under Seth's leadership, EdgeTech has grown from a startup vision to a trusted technology partner serving over 500 clients across multiple industries. His expertise in cloud architecture, cybersecurity, and digital transformation has positioned EdgeTech as a leader in the IT services sector. Seth's commitment to continuous learning and adaptation has ensured that EdgeTech stays ahead of technological trends, providing clients with tomorrow's solutions today.
